About

A warm glow that feels like home

Vale Beach Bar is a modern beach haven crafted for locals and travelers alike. We blend slow mornings, golden afternoons, and electric evenings into a single effortless rhythm inspired by the Vlore coastline.

Elegant, yet unpretentious

Soft sand terraces, bronze-lit cabanas, and a curated artful soundtrack set the tone for every visit.

Human touch

Our crew orchestrates welcome drinks, bespoke playlists, and thoughtful moments so you can simply arrive and savor the view.

Reservations

Secure your evenings on the sand
